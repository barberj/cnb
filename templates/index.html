Welcome to Creating New Beginnings Counseling and Consulting Services. My goal is to provide clients with new insights to thought processes and behaviors along with new methods for handling every day difficulties that can be utilized right away. I hope to provide my clients with a safe and inviting environment where they can learn more about the world around them and build on their strengths. It is important that we work together to formulate treatment goals, and use the counseling process to understand and improve social and emotional functioning.

The process looks like the following:

  1. Identify the core problem(s).
  2. Develop together an individualized realistic treatment plan.
  3. Offer proven techniques.
  4. Applicable information, guidance & support necessary to meet to your goals.
