Life is full of interpersonal and emotional challenges. Sometimes these periods of uncertainty and challenge can be frightening. However, I believe they provide an opportunity to grow, attain inner peace and create a new beginning. My goal is to provide a safe and confidential space in which you can explore your personal concerns in order to develop and attain personal goals, achieve authentic relationships and gain self awareness.

Adolescents

Counseling services to help children and their families with:
  • Behavioral problems
  • Academic struggles
  • Social skill deficits
  • Friendship issues
  • Family conflict
  • Worries / Anxiety / Stress
  • Low self-esteem
  • Separation and Divorce Issues
I enjoy the challenge of adolescent counseling & helping teens navigate the tricky waters of middle school and high school. Being an adolescent is not easy; managing schoolwork, peer pressure, sports, relationships, electronics, driving, and college applications can be overwhelming. Conflict around these issues can put teens and their parents at risk for depression and anxiety.

I work to help teens appreciate their parents and help parents celebrate the launching of their teens. I teach adolescents social and life skills that will serve them throughout their life in schools, families, and in the job market.

Therapy for Individuals

Common concerns brought by individual clients include symptoms of:
  • Relationship Struggles
  • Work Stress including Underemployment / Unemployment
  • Anxiety
  • Grief
  • Low Self-Esteem
Issues such as these are handled in a compassionate, non-blaming approach. Therapy is focused on solutions and attempts to bring about short-term relief as well as long-term change.
The adults that I work with are successful, smart, and determined to work on the one or two areas that are not working in their lives. I partner with them in building more satisfying personal lives, relationships, and careers. I see people who have tried everything and have made the courageous decision to admit they need some help.

Nothing is more frustrating than trying really hard and still feeling like the particular problem is not getting better. I help my clients learn new approaches, so they don't simply try hard, but actually see progress. Most clients report they wish they had started the process sooner!
